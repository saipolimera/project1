    • The WatchMeMommy Story

    Hello. Welcome to WatchMeMommy. So glad you’re here.

    It all started with the vision of connecting today’s busy families using modern technology. we want to erase the well known confession from parents, "They're growing up and we missing it".

    This service was designed to help today’s busy families stay in touch with their children's day to day activities and precious moments that would otherwise be missed. From parents at work to grandparents across the country or even aunts or uncles overseas, the WatchMeMommy service brings happiness by making your family together.

    WatchMeMommy is an online video streaming service designed specifically to support childcare centers. WatchMeMommy provides security camera systems with video streaming and parent monitoring features to the childcare centers.Families have the peace of mind that they are able to see their child from anywhere in the world.

    What is WatchMeMommy?

    WatchMeMommy is an online video streaming service designed specifically to support childcare centers. WatchMeMommy benefits both childcare business owners and their customers i.e., parents of children. WatchMeMommy was created to keep families close and form trust between the family and the childcare center.
